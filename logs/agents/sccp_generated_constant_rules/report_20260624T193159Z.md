## Structural Change

- Added registry-owned SCCP constant rule lattices:
  - `sccp_constant_seed_rules`
  - `sccp_constant_eval_rules`
- Extended `tools/gen_op_kinds.py` to validate and render:
  - `SccpConstantSeedRule`
  - `SccpConstantEvalRule`

  - `opcode_sccp_constant_eval_rule_table`

- Regenerated `docs/design/foundation/STRUCTURAL_AUDIT_BOARD.md`; the ratchet now reports 495 findings with 4 improved metrics.
